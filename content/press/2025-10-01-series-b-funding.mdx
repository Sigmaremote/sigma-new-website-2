Major investment round will accelerate product development and market expansion as Sigma continues to lead the global workforce management revolution.

## Investment Highlights

- **$25M Series B** funding led by Andreessen Horowitz
- **Participation from existing investors** including Sequoia Capital
- **Expansion into new markets** across Asia-Pacific and Europe
- **Enhanced AI capabilities** for compliance automation

This funding round positions Sigma to become the global standard for workforce management, enabling companies worldwide to hire and pay talent anywhere with confidence and compliance.
